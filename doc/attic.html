Issue (1) 'IE drawing outside canvas'

If you paint over the borders of the canvas, expect two behaviours (Issue # 20140828.0541) :

  • The object is painted even outside the canvas borders. This is what IE does.
     
  • The object outside the canvas borders is clipped. This do Chrome, FF and Opera. One question is, whether for the outborder parts, the calculations are done or skipped. The answer were interesting for resource and performance understanding.
